üß© PF Insight

This case shows that cruelty is not gender-exclusive. Digital environments like dating apps amplify depersonalization and cruelty on all sides. People act based on perceived reality, not objective reality. The woman had believed men were exaggerating. Only by stepping into their shoes did she recalibrate her perception.

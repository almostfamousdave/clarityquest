PF Trilemma Tool: You Can Only Pick Two

“Where there are three desirable outcomes, choosing two means sacrificing the third. That’s not failure, that’s clarity.”

🧠 Purpose

This tool helps you form and analyze Trilemmas, scenarios where you or others expect to achieve three conflicting goals at once. It’s a clarity exercise used to:

  • Detect distorted expectations
  • Reveal unconscious bias
  • Clarify values
  • Raise your Perception Factor (PF)

🛠️ How to Use This Tool

1. Spot the Unrealistic Expectation

Look for promises, plans, or beliefs that claim: “We can have all three!”

Write them down in this format:

  • [Outcome A]
  • [Outcome B]
  • [Outcome C]

Example:

  • Absolute Security
  • Full Personal Freedom
  • Total Government Transparency

2. Test the Tension

Ask yourself:

  • “Can all three coexist without trade-offs?”
  • “Which two actually matter most in this context?”
  • “Which one am I pretending I don’t have to sacrifice?”

3. Choose Your Two

Make your values conscious:

  • ✅ Keep: [Outcome A]
  • ✅ Keep: [Outcome B]
  • ❌ Sacrifice: [Outcome C]

Explain why this is the best trade-off for truth, clarity, and long-term reality alignment.

4. PF Check-In

Use these PF questions to reflect:

  • Am I resisting trade-offs because they’re emotionally uncomfortable?
  • Is someone else hiding the trade-off to manipulate me?
  • Am I choosing comfort or tribal loyalty over clarity?
  • If I had to face the third loss, what emotion would I need to process?

🔄 Common PF Trilemmas

TrilemmaPick TwoSacrifice
Love, Truth, BelongingBelonging + TruthLove may be rejected
Speed, Quality, AffordabilitySpeed + AffordabilityQuality suffers
Security, Liberty, TransparencyLiberty + SecurityTransparency drops
Comfort, Growth, ClarityGrowth + ClarityComfort is gone
Control, Trust, PeaceControl + TrustPeace may vanish

These aren’t moral judgments. They’re trade-offs made visible.

🧭 PF Trilemma in Daily Life

Use when:

  • Making major decisions
  • Feeling emotionally conflicted
  • Evaluating political messaging
  • Challenging naive idealism
  • Detecting manipulation

🧱 PF Foundation Tie-In

This tool raises PF by training your ability to:

  • Spot illusions
  • Tolerate uncomfortable truth
  • Recognize systemic constraints
  • Disarm “have-it-all” thinking

“You don’t rise in PF by being smarter. You rise by refusing to lie to yourself.”

🧪 Optional Journal Prompt

“What’s a trilemma I’ve been denying exists? Which one am I pretending I don’t have to sacrifice?”

✨ Closing Thought

Trilemmas aren’t traps. They’re tests of clarity.

A low PF person demands all three. A rising PF person sees the trade-offs. A high PF person embraces the sacrifice required to live in truth.

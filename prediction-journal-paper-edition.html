📚 Supplement Modules

Use these pages only when patterns of distortion repeat.

Intellectual Domain Check

Use when your logic, confidence, or reasoning may be flawed.

  • Did I challenge my assumptions?
  • Did I seek disconfirming information?
  • Did I confuse familiarity with truth?
  • PF Triggers:
    ☐ Certainty without data

Moral Domain Check

Use when justifications or rationalizations may be overriding conscience.

  • Am I excusing something wrong for comfort or gain?
  • Does this align with long-term moral clarity or just short-term convenience?
  • Would I want this behavior/choice made public?

    ☐ Moral relativism
    ☐ Justifying harm
    ☐ Compromising integrity

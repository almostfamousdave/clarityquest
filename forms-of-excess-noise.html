Forms of Excess Noise

Overview

In the Perception Factor (PF) Framework, Noise is the distortion that separates perception from reality. It can come from emotional triggers, cultural framing, ideological indoctrination, trauma, or even overconfidence. When noise increases, PF decreases.

Excess Noise is not just distracting, it corrupts clarity.

This note outlines the most common types of Excess Noise, their symptoms, effects on PF, and how to disarm them.

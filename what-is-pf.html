What is PF?

Imagine two people standing side by side, looking at the same event, yet coming to completely different conclusions. One sees clearly. The other sees through a fog of emotion, bias, fear, or cultural distortion.

Why?

Because their Perception Factor, their ability to accurately perceive reality, is different.
